FAQ

What makes Dew Botanicals different?

We use only pure, natural ingredients sourced from Sri Lanka's rich botanical heritage. Our products are handcrafted in small batches to ensure the highest quality and freshness.

Are your products suitable for all skin types?

Yes! Our gentle, natural formulations work beautifully with all skin types. The Island Ritual Cleanser and Ceylon Dew Toner are designed to balance and nourish without irritation.

How should I use the products?

Start with the Island Ritual Cleanser on damp skin, massage gently, and rinse. Follow with the Ceylon Dew Toner applied with a cotton pad or sprayed directly onto skin. Use morning and evening for best results.

Are your products vegan and cruelty-free?

Absolutely. All Dew Botanicals products are 100% vegan, cruelty-free, and made with plant-based ingredients. We never test on animals.

What is your shipping policy?

We offer shipping across Sri Lanka with delivery within 3-5 business days. International shipping is available upon request. All orders are carefully packaged to ensure your products arrive in perfect condition.

Can I return products if I'm not satisfied?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, contact us within 14 days for a full refund or exchange.
