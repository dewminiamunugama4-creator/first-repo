About Us

We craft island-born skincare that is pure, gentle, and effective — inspired by Sri Lanka’s botanicals and the rhythm of the tropics.

Natural

100% plant-based ingredients

Sustainable

Eco-friendly packaging

Cruelty-Free

At Dew Botanicals, we believe skincare should be simple, nourishing, and rooted in nature. Our formulas are thoughtfully created using Sri Lankan botanicals such as moringa, coconut, and cinnamon — ingredients cherished for centuries for their restorative properties.


Each product is made in small batches to ensure freshness and potency. We work closely with local partners to source responsibly and package consciously, so your ritual feels good for your skin and the planet.


From our home at 7°N, we share an island philosophy of balance: between land and sea, tradition and innovation. Welcome to your new daily ritual.
